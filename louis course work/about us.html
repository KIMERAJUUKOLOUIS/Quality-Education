About JK FOUNDED SCHOOLS

This project was funded by a group of passionate educators in 2015.Frustated by lack of access to affordable learning, they built a platform that put students first. what begun as a tutoring center in a small town has now grown into a global education movement.

MISSION

To support students of all backgrounds by offering innovative, personalized and engaging learning experiences.

What We Stand For.

  • GROWTH:Help students build skills for life.
  • EQUITY:We priotize inclusive learning environment.
  • INNOVATION:We use mordern tools and methods to teach.
